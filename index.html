Humphreys Hill House was dreamt into existence by a vision whose reverence for Gusii tradition meets refined design. Every arch, wall, and window whispers heritage while offering modern ease.

Perched atop the lush Kiong'anyo hill, this boutique sanctuary welcomes you to indulge in warmth, wisdom, and quiet elegance. It’s not just a place to stay—it’s a space to belong.
